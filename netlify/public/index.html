How it works

Set up once. Protected forever.

No maps to check. No buttons to press. ChargeShield works invisibly so you never have to think about it.

01
Download & allow location
Install ChargeShield and grant background location access. One permission. That's all setup done.
02
We watch the boundaries
Our precise GPS engine monitors all London charge zone boundaries in real time, 24/7 — even when your phone's locked.
03
Instant alert on entry
The moment you cross into a zone, you get a push notification with the charge amount and a direct link to pay.
04
Final deadline reminder
Haven't paid by evening? We send a last reminder so you never wake up to a penalty notice on your doormat.
