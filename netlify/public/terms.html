1. About ChargeShield

ChargeShield is an informational mobile application that uses GPS technology to detect when a user's device enters the approximate area of known UK road charging zones (including but not limited to the London Congestion Charge Zone, Ultra Low Emission Zone, Low Emission Zone, Dartford Crossing, Silvertown Tunnel and Blackwall Tunnel). The app sends push notifications to alert users of potential charging zone entries.
