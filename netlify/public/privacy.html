Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 March 2026 | Version 1.0

ChargeShield ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data when you use the ChargeShield app and website at chargeshield.co.uk.

2. Location Data

ChargeShield requires background location access to detect zone entries while the app is not in the foreground. This is the core function of the app.

  • Location data is processed entirely on your device
  • We do not transmit, store or sell your location data to any server or third party
  • Location history is stored locally on your device only
  • You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings, though this will prevent the app from functioning

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Email address — to send your Pro licence key and important service notifications
  • Payment data — processed by Stripe to manage your subscription (we do not store card details)
  • Location data — processed locally on your device to detect zone entries and trigger notifications

4. Third Party Services

We use the following trusted third party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/gb/privacy)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (resend.com/privacy)
  • Firebase (Google) — push notification delivery (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Netlify — website hosting (netlify.com/privacy)

We do not sell your data to any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

  • Email address — retained while your account is active and for 12 months after cancellation
  • Payment records — retained as required by law (typically 6 years for financial records)
  • Location and journey data — stored on your device only; deleted when you uninstall the app

6. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@chargeshield.co.uk.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children

ChargeShield is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
